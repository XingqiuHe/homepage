Xingqiu He

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), focusing on Edge Computing and Semantic Communication. Before that, I earned both my Bachelor's and Ph.D. degrees at the University of Electronic Science and Technology (UESTC). From December 2018 to June 2019, I joined Huawei Noah's Ark Lab as an intern, participating in exploratory research about neural architecture search.
